Helpful Tips

- Certify all documents before submission.
- Missing documents can delay the process.
- For self-employed permits, include proof of investment and incorporation certificates.

Permit Requirements

The permit allows non-citizens to legally work and reside in the country under a single authorization.

Attachments
  • Certified passport copy
  • Two passport-sized photos
  • Proof of payment for long-term permit
  • Employment contract
  • Certified academic documents
  • Applicant's CV
  • Medical report
  • Letter from employer
  • Covering letter from employer
  • Application letter
  • Birth certificate
  • Missing document explanation
  • References or testimonials
  • Explicit advert copy
If Applicable
  • Marriage certificate
  • Divorce papers
  • Support for dependents
If Self-Employed or Investor
  • Share certificate
  • Proof of investments
  • Incorporation certificate
