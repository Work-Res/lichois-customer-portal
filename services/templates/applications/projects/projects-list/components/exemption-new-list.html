Helpful Tips

- Certify all documents before submission.
- Missing documents can delay the process.
- For self-employed permits, include proof of investment and incorporation certificates.

Permit Requirements

The permit allows non-citizens to legally work in the country under a single authorization.

Attachments
  • Certified passport copy
  • Two passport-sized photos
  • Offer letter if changing employment
  • Cover letter from the employer
  • A manual certificate
  • Certified academic documents
  • Medical report
  • Birth certificate
  • Missing birth certificate explanation
  • References or testimonials
  • A copy of employment contract
  • A copy of applicant cv
  • A copy of advert
  • An application letter
  • Copies of passport pages showing recent entry stamps (fresh applications)

If Applicable
  • Marriage certificate
  • Divorce papers
  • Covering letter from the supporter/principal applicant for each dependants
  • Certified copy of valid passport for each dependants
  • Passport photo for each dependants
  • Certified on reverse passport photo for each dependants
  • School admission letters for children
