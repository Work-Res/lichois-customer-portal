Helpful Tips

- Certify all documents before submission.
- Missing documents can delay the process.

Permit Requirements
Attachments
  • Certified passport copy
  • Two passport-sized photos
  • A manual certificate
If Applicable
  • Letter of support from mission/spouse/supporter
  • Proof of means of support
  • Proof of mission registration with Registrar of Societies
  • Bank statement
  • Confirmation of employment by the employer/reference letter
  • Valid and certified copies of title deed/lease agreement
  • Tax Return (BURS) through the employer
  • Valuation of shares
  • Share certificate
  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • List of directors
  • Annual returns
  • Financial statement
  • Trading license
  • Tax clearance
  • Company profile
  • List of assets and valuation
